## Company Story (About section)
In September 2018, reconstruction began in the Angren Free Economic Zone — a joint venture between Uzbek and Chinese partners with one goal: manufacture steel pipes meeting international standards on Uzbekistan's soil.

By December 2019, the first ERW production line was operational. Since then: continuous modernization, expanding capacity, refining processes year after year.

Today, 160+ local workers operate certified facilities. Partnerships with ArcelorMittal, EVRAZ, HUAYE, and MMK strengthen the entire Uzbek metals industry. Every product is GOST-certified. Environmental responsibility is built in, not bolted on.
